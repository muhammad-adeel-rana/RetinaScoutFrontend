About Us

Retinal diseases are a leading cause of preventable blindness globally, yet traditional manual screening is often labor-intensive and inconsistent. RetinaScout addresses this challenge as an automated image analysis system designed to support ophthalmologists.

Instead of relying solely on manual observation, our platform utilizes advanced deep learning technology to identify pathological features with high precision. By digitizing the clinical workflow, we aim to reduce the workload on medical professionals and improve patient outcomes through faster, more reliable diagnostics.
